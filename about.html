Meet Peter Cole

Entrepreneur, web designer & developer, and multi-channel marketing strategist with 20+ years of experience — based in Nashville, Tennessee.

The Story

I started Sites In A Day because I saw too many small businesses stuck without a web presence — not because they didn't want one, but because the process felt too slow, too expensive, or too confusing. I knew there had to be a better way.

With over two decades of experience spanning web design, development, and multi-channel marketing strategy, I built a system that combines AI-accelerated design with hands-on professional refinement. The result: custom, high-quality websites delivered in 24 hours, starting at just $297.

Every site we build is unique. No cookie-cutter templates. No corners cut. Just fast, professional work that helps your business show up and stand out online.

My Background

I've spent my career at the intersection of design, development, and marketing. As a Senior Web Developer at Infinity Medical Marketing for 10 years, I honed my craft building high-performance websites and digital experiences in a fast-paced agency environment.

At Brand Journalists, I rose through multiple leadership roles — from Senior Web Developer and Creative Director to Chief Marketing Officer and Chief Operating Officer — leading teams to drive results through effective digital marketing strategies and customer acquisition.

I also founded Keyhole Press, a creative venture I ran for over a decade, and served as Marketing Director at Perimeter Park Executive Center. Every role sharpened my ability to understand what businesses need to grow online.
