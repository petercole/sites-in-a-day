Our Story

Since 2018, Velvet & Vintage has been curating the finest vintage and preloved fashion pieces from around the world. Each item in our collection is carefully selected for its quality, uniqueness, and timeless appeal.

We believe fashion should be sustainable, authentic, and personal. Our expertly sourced collection spans decades of style, from classic 1950s silhouettes to rare 90s pieces, allowing you to express your individual aesthetic.

Every piece comes with its own narrative, waiting to become part of your story.
