Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common legal questions.

How much does it cost to hire your firm?
+

We offer flexible billing arrangements including hourly rates, fixed fees, and contingency options depending on your matter. Most initial consultations are complimentary. During your consultation, we provide transparent estimates of anticipated costs and discuss payment structures that work best for your situation.

What is your process for estate planning?
+

We begin with a comprehensive discovery meeting to understand your family structure, assets, and goals. We then recommend appropriate documents and strategies, draft all necessary paperwork, and coordinate execution and funding. Regular reviews ensure your plan stays current with tax law changes and life circumstances.

How long will my matter take to resolve?
+

Timeline varies significantly depending on the complexity of your matter. Estate planning typically takes 2-4 weeks from initial meeting to finalized documents. Business transactions can range from 4-12 weeks. Litigation timelines depend on case complexity, opposing parties' cooperation, and court schedules. We'll provide realistic projections early in our engagement.

Do you represent businesses outside Chicago?
+

Yes, we represent clients throughout Illinois and can assist with multi-state matters. For clients in other states, we can work with local counsel or provide limited scope representation depending on your needs and the specific legal issues involved.

What makes Hartwell & Associates different?
+

We combine deep expertise with genuine client relationships. Our partners maintain active practices and personally manage significant matters—you don't work through associates. We prioritize strategic thinking over billable hours, maintain transparent communication, and deliver results that exceed expectations.
