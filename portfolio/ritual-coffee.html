Our Story

Crafted with Passion, Served with Soul

Born from a deep love of exceptional coffee, Ritual was founded with one simple belief: every cup should be an experience. We source our beans directly from small-lot farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, building relationships that honor both the craft and the farmer.

Our roasting process is meticulous — small batches roasted in-house, tasted daily, and brewed by baristas who've spent years mastering their craft. This isn't just coffee. It's a ritual.

— Marcus & Elena, Founders

From Bean to Cup

Our Roasting Process

Three pillars that define every cup we serve.

01

Direct Sourcing

We travel to origin farms each season, selecting only the top 2% of specialty-grade beans. Every bag is fully traceable from farm to cup.

02

Small-Batch Roasting

Roasted in 12kg batches on our Probat roaster, each profile is developed to highlight the bean's natural character. Never over-roasted.

03

Precision Brewing

Our baristas dial in each morning, calibrating grind, dose, and extraction to serve every cup at its absolute peak.
