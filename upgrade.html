Frequently Asked Questions

Will my domain name change? ▼

No. Your domain stays exactly the same. We rebuild your site while keeping your URL intact. Your customers won't notice anything except that your site looks amazing now.

What about my existing content? ▼

We'll migrate any content you want to keep — pages, images, text, etc. You tell us what matters, and we transfer it to your new site. Anything outdated can be removed or completely redesigned.

How is 24 hours even possible? ▼

We've built the process. We start immediately, use modern tools and frameworks, and focus on what matters. No complexity, no waiting. When you order a SitesInADay rebuild, you're getting a dedicated team sprinting to launch your new site fast.

What if I need changes after launch? ▼

You get revision rounds included (1 in Starter, 3 in Launch, unlimited in Growth). After launch, we provide email support to help with any questions. For future changes, we offer ongoing support packages.

Will my site be SEO optimized? ▼

Yes. All SitesInADay sites include SEO fundamentals: fast loading, mobile responsiveness, proper heading structure, meta tags, and clean code. Launch and Growth packages include advanced SEO optimization to help you rank better in search.

Do I need to do anything during the 24 hours? ▼

Just be available for a quick kickoff call at the start to share your current site and your vision. Then we handle everything. You'll review the finished site before we go live.
