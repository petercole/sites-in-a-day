Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What do I need to get started?
+
Just tell us about your business! Fill out our brief form with your business name, what you do, and your goals. We'll handle all the technical details.
How does 24-hour delivery really work?
+
Our AI accelerates the design and development process by generating a foundation in hours. Our expert team then refines, optimizes, and launches it. You get a professional site without the typical weeks-long timeline.
Can I edit my site after launch?
+
Absolutely! We provide either a simple admin panel or hand off the source code so you can make updates yourself. For the Launch and Growth packages, we include support to help with changes.
What platform do you build on?
+
We build custom HTML/CSS/JavaScript sites deployed to modern hosting. This means faster loading, better SEO, and lower costs than WordPress or template builders. You own your site completely.
Do you offer ongoing support?
+
Yes! The Launch package includes 30 days of support, and Growth includes 60 days. After that, you can purchase support as needed, or we can recommend affordable hosting and maintenance options.
What if I'm not happy with the site?
+
Your satisfaction is guaranteed. If you're not happy, we'll revise until you are (within your package's revision rounds). Still not satisfied? We offer a 100% money-back guarantee, no questions asked.
